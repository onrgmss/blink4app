Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

Blink4! ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.
  • Game Data: We store your game scores, achievements, and progress.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers for analytics and advertising purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game service
  • Display leaderboards and competitive features
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Show relevant advertisements
  • Communicate with you about updates or support

Advertising

Blink4! uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Database)
  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Apple Sign In (iOS Authentication)
  • Google Sign In (Authentication)

Children's Privacy

Blink4! is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Export your game data

To delete your account, go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account within the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
